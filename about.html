Meet Wendy Ashman.

She’s the owner and creative mastermind behind WDesigns. For the past fifteen-plus years, she has been working in home staging and design for the industry’s biggest names, never giving anything less than her 100% attention to even the smallest of details. And now, she’s bringing her design expertise out of the showroom, and through your front door.

Wendy and WDesigns bring beauty into your living spaces, and happiness into your life. Let’s build a space to create memories.
